Management Information

The smooth operation of Greggs is made possible by management members. Your duty is to ensure the game runs professionally and assist players.

Scenarios: Trollers

Remain composed and professional. Use the following commands in order:

:warn [User] Trolling (Warn twice)
:kick [User] Trolling after warnings
:serverban [User] Persistent Trolling

Exploiters

Contact the CEO or Vice CEO immediately. Take evidence (video/screenshots) as soon as possible.

Management Ranks

Store Regional Manager

Permitted to hold community shifts. No rank cooldown. Hard work leads to quick promotion.

Bakery Supervisor

Experienced hosts. 3-week rank cooldown applies before next promotion.

Store Manager

Uppermost management position. Can oversee any session or occurrence. 4-week rank cooldown.

Promotion Guidelines

What NOT to do:

Begging/Hinting: Asking for a promotion results in immediate termination. No exceptions.

What TO do:

  • Being extremely active in-game.
  • Assisting other management members.
  • Hosting numerous successful shifts.
  • Handling scenarios professionally.
