Information about Management

As you are aware, we have management here at GREGGS. The smooth operation of the game is made possible by those management members.

Duties of Management:

Your duties are simple. As listed before, you are required to help the game to go smoothly. We gave you permission to give some special commands. All those commands will be listed in the next sections.

Scenarios:

We have a warning system on GREGGS in case you encounter any problems. Keep your composure and behave professionally. These things do happen, so you should respond appropriately. All the potential situations are listed here, along with advice on how to handle them.

In every game, there are trollers. They are suspects who have the viewpoint of an unprofessional person who hurts people in a place and who wishes to portray the game in the worst possible light, even though that is not how the game actually looks. In situations like this, the management staff should always remain composed and act professionally.

/warn [Suspect] Trolling (Warn twice)
/kick [Suspect] Trolling after warnings
/serverban [Suspect] Persistent Trolling

Exploiters: You ought to contact the Chief Executive Officer or the Vice Chief Executive Officer right away. As soon as you can, you should take evidence. Although we don't anticipate this happening, this is to stop any kind of exploit from getting into our game.

Any small matter: Like a rude member, harassment of any kind, or anything that puts GREGGS in a terrible situation. The standard warning system should be used in conjunction with it. A server ban follows three warnings, followed by a kick.

Ranks of Management

Our management team uses a point system that is ranked by rankings. You will have a reduced quota and certain rank bonuses with each rank, but there is also a rank cooldown for each rank.

Store Regional Manager:

The management staff has never seen them before. They are permitted to hold community shifts. When you reach this rank, there won't be a rank cooldown, so if you put in a lot of effort and the headquarters saw it, you have a good possibility of getting promoted.

Bakery Supervisor:

They have a great deal of hosting experience and are somewhat knowledgeable about this aspect of our game. We demand the finest of the best when you are promoted to this level. You can move up to the next rank after your three-week rank cooldown has ended.

Store Manager:

It is the uppermost position in management. They are permitted to observe any training or shift. Furthermore, they occasionally have the ability to oversee any occurrence! Everything they do is extremely private. You have no idea what we're doing at the moment! They have a four-week rank cooldown.

Within GREGGS, we have all of these ranks. You should put in a lot of effort to obtain them all!

Guidelines of Promotion

For those who would prefer to be promoted more quickly and easily, this area of the management rules is crucial. Within GREGGS you have to meet some requirements before being recommended by the GREGGS headquarters.

Which things you should NOT do?

Begging for a promotion is prohibited. One element of the disciplinary guidelines, specifically “Hinting (promotions),” automatically results in termination from our organization. This is an extraordinary occurrence in our organization, and no exceptions or tolerance will be granted. You should be patient and work hard.

Which things should you do?

Being extremely active in the game, assisting other management members, and hosting numerous shifts. As well handle other situations that you are required to be there. You will be heard by the headquarters and may be elevated by the headquarters themselves or automatically!
