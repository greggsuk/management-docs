Information about Management

As you are aware, we have management here at GREGGS. The smooth operation of the game is made possible by those management members.

Duties of Management:

Your duties are simple. As listed before, you are required to help the game to go smoothly. We gave you permission to give some special commands. All those commands will be listed in the next sections.

Scenarios:

We have a warning system on GREGGS in case you encounter any problems. Keep your composure and behave professionally. These things do happen, so you should respond appropriately.

Trollers: They are suspects who have the viewpoint of an unprofessional person who hurts people in a place. You should remain composed and act professionally.

/warn [User] (Twice) -> /kick [User] -> /serverban [User]

Exploiters: You ought to contact the CEO or Vice CEO right away. Take evidence immediately.

Small matters: Rude members or harassment. Use the standard warning system: 3 warnings -> Kick -> Server Ban.

Ranks of Management

Store Regional Manager:

Permitted to hold community shifts. No rank cooldown. If you put in a lot of effort and HQ sees it, you have a good possibility of getting promoted.

Bakery Supervisor:

Great hosting experience. We demand the finest of the best. You can move up after your three-week rank cooldown has ended.

Store Manager:

Uppermost position in management. Permitted to observe any training or shift. Extremely private tasks. They have a four-week rank cooldown.

Guidelines of Promotion

Which things you should NOT do?

Begging for a promotion is prohibited. “Hinting (promotions)” automatically results in termination. No exceptions or tolerance will be granted.

Which things should you do?

Being extremely active in the game, assisting other management members, and hosting numerous shifts. You will be heard by the headquarters and may be elevated automatically!
