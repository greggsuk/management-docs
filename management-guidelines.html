Ranks of Management

Store Regional Manager

Permitted to hold community shifts. No rank cooldown. High effort leads to headquarters promotion.

Bakery Supervisor

Hosting experts. Three-week rank cooldown required before next promotion eligibility.

Store Manager

Uppermost position. Observes any training/shift. Handles private occurrences. Four-week rank cooldown.

Guidelines of Promotion

What NOT to do

Begging for a promotion is prohibited. “Hinting (promotions)” results in automatic TERMINATION. No exceptions.

What YOU should do

Be active, assist other members, and host numerous shifts. Handle situations professionally to be noticed by Headquarters.
