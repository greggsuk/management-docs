Information about Management

As you are aware, we have management here at GREGGS. The smooth operation of the game is made possible by those management members.

Duties of Management

Your duties are simple. As listed before, you are required to help the game to go smoothly. We gave you permission to give some special commands. All those commands will be listed in the next sections.

Scenarios & Protocol

We have a warning system on GREGGS in case you encounter any problems. Keep your composure and behave professionally. All the potential situations are listed here:

Trollers

Trollers are suspects who wish to portray the game in the worst possible light. Management should always remain composed.

1. Use :warn [Suspect] (Twice) 2. If behavior continues, use :kick [Suspect] 3. If they re-join and carry on, use :serverban [Suspect]

Exploiters

Exploiters are common in supermarket games. You ought to contact the Chief Executive Officer or the Vice Chief Executive Officer right away. Take evidence immediately to stop exploits from spreading.

Small Matters

Rude members, harassment, or anything that puts GREGGS in a terrible situation: Standard system: 3 warnings -> Kick -> Server Ban.

Always ask for assistance in the management chat channel if you need it for any of these situations.

Ranks of Management

Our management team uses a point system. You will have a reduced quota and rank bonuses with each rank, but there is also a rank cooldown.

Store Regional Manager

Permitted to hold community shifts. There is no rank cooldown for this rank. High possibility of promotion if HQ sees effort.

Bakery Supervisor

Requires high hosting experience. We demand the finest of the best. Promotion eligibility opens after the three-week rank cooldown.

Store Manager

The uppermost position in management. Permitted to observe any training or shift. Everything they do is extremely private. They have a four-week rank cooldown.

Guidelines of Promotion

You have to meet some requirements before being recommended by the GREGGS headquarters.

What NOT to do

Begging for a promotion is prohibited. Specifically "Hinting (promotions)" automatically results in termination from our organization. No exceptions or tolerance will be granted.

What YOU should do

  • Being extremely active in the game.
  • Assisting other management members.
  • Hosting numerous shifts.
  • Handling situations where you are required.

You will be heard by headquarters and may be elevated manually or automatically!
