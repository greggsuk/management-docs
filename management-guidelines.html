Information about Management

GENERAL_DUTIES.TXT
As you are aware, we have management here at GREGGS. The smooth operation of the game is made possible by those management members.

Your duties are simple. As listed before, you are required to help the game to go smoothly. We gave you permission to give some special commands. All those commands will be listed in the next sections.

Scenarios & Protocol

SCENARIO_HANDLER.SH
# TROLLERS:
In every game, there are trollers. They are suspects who have the viewpoint of an unprofessional person who hurts people in a place and who wishes to portray the game in the worst possible light.

Management should always remain composed.
1. Use “:warn” twice to warn the suspect.
2. If trolling continues, use “:kick”.
3. If they re-join and carry on, use “:serverban”.

# EXPLOITERS:
Contact the CEO or Vice CEO right away. Take evidence as soon as possible to stop exploits in our game.

# SMALL MATTERS:
(Rude members, harassment, etc.)
Standard warning system: 3 Warnings -> Kick -> Server Ban.

# SUPPORT:
Always ask for assistance in the management chat channel if needed.
