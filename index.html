Management Shift Guidelines

The purpose of this document is to ensure that all Greggs UK staff members deliver a high-quality experience for our customers. Adherence to these guidelines is mandatory for all Greggs UK Management personnel.

What is the main objective of shifts?

  • Maintain operational excellence within Greggs UK while supervising personnel.
  • Identify and recognize the Star of the Shift (SOTS).
  • Facilitate promotion recommendations for deserving low-command members if a Headquarters member is present.
Your role as a management member is crucial in upholding Greggs UK standards. Always supervise those attending your shift with professionalism.

Announcement Time

Before beginning the session, the Host must alert the server via the command bar.

Protocol: You are strictly permitted to execute the announcement command a maximum of 3 times per shift.
sannounce [Your Message]

Starting Time

Adhere to the following dialogue exactly. Consistency is key to Greggs UK Management standards.

Host Dialogue:

"Welcome to today's shift session."
"I am [Name], and I will be acting as your Host for today's session."

Co-Host Dialogue:

"I am [Name], and I will be your Co-Host for today's session."

Session Rules:

"Before we proceed with role assignments, please listen carefully to these rules:"
"1. You must remain at your assigned station at all times."
"2. Professionalism is required throughout the entire session."
"3. Do not request role changes without prior permission."

"With that being said, [Co-Host Name] and I will now assign your roles."

Roles Assignment

Assign each attendee exactly one role. Do not exceed the capacity limits established for each role.

Dismissal:

"You are now dismissed. Please proceed to your designated stations immediately."

Ending Time

Once the shift concludes, the management team must regroup to evaluate performance.

  • Discuss the selection for Star of the Shift.
  • Evaluate any promotion recommendations for the Headquarters team.
"All staff members, please report to the shift room once your area is cleared."

Star of the Shift (SOTS)

The Star of the Shift recognition process does not require a fixed dialogue. You may announce it in a style that encourages the staff.

Reward the individual who demonstrated the highest level of professionalism and efficiency during the session.

Dismissal Time

Following the SOTS announcement, formally dismiss all personnel.

"You are officially dismissed. Thank you for your hard work and for attending this Greggs UK shift. We hope to see you at the next one!"
