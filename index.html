Management Shift Guidelines

The purpose of this document is to ensure that all Greggs UK staff members deliver a high-quality experience for our customers. Adherence to these guidelines is mandatory for all Greggs UK Management personnel.

Shift Roles

Each role within the shift has specific requirements and responsibilities that must be followed strictly.

Role Name Rank Requirement Tasks & Responsibilities Limit
Host Store Regional Manager+ Assigns staff roles and oversees the entire session performance. 1
Co-Host Store Regional Manager+ Assists the host and manages ground operations/staff flow. 1
Overseer Store Manager+ (HQ Perms) Monitors the shift and ensures management follows protocol. 1
POS Handler Any Rank+ Handles transactions at the registers and assists customers. 4
Orders Handler Any Rank+ Responsible for preparing and serving all food/drink orders. 3
Greeter Any Rank+ Welcomes customers and directs them to available registers. 2

Announcement Time

Before beginning the session, the Host must alert the server via the command bar.

/sannounce

Starting Time

Host & Co-Host Dialogue:

"Welcome to today's shift session."
"I am [Name], and I will be acting as your Host for today's session."
"I am [Name], and I will be your Co-Host for today's session."

Roles Assignment

"You are now dismissed. Please proceed to your designated stations immediately."

Ending Time

Once the shift concludes, the management team must regroup to evaluate performance.

"All staff members, please report to the shift room once your area is cleared."

Star of the Shift (SOTS)

Recognize the individual who showed the best performance during the session.

Dismissal Time

Once the Star of the Shift has been recognized, you must formally conclude the session.

Final Dismissal Dialogue:

"You are officially dismissed. Thank you for your hard work and for attending this Greggs UK shift. We hope to see you at the next one!"
