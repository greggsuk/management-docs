Management Shift Guidelines

The purpose of this document is to ensure that all Greggs UK staff members deliver a high-quality experience for our customers. Adherence to these guidelines is mandatory for all Greggs UK Management personnel.

Shift Roles

Role NameRank / TasksLimit
HostStore Regional Manager+. Assigns staff roles and oversees the entire session.1
Co-HostStore Regional Manager+. Assists the host and manages ground operations.1
OverseerStore Manager+ (with HQ permission). Monitors the entire shift.1
POS HandlerAny rank+. Handles transactions and assists customers.4
Orders HandlerAny rank+. Responsible for preparing all order requests.3
GreeterAny rank+. Welcomes customers upon entry to the bakery.2

Announcement Time

Before beginning the session, the Host must alert the server via the command bar.

/sannounce

Starting Time

Host & Co-Host Dialogue:

"Welcome to today's shift session."
"I am [Name], and I will be acting as your Host for today's session."
"I am [Name], and I will be your Co-Host for today's session."

Session Rules:

"1. You must remain at your assigned station at all times."
"2. Professionalism is required throughout the entire session."
"3. Do not request role changes without prior permission."

"With that being said, [Co-Host Name] and I will now assign your roles."

Roles Assignment

"You are now dismissed. Please proceed to your designated stations immediately."

Ending Time

Once the shift concludes, the management team must regroup to evaluate performance.

"All staff members, please report to the shift room once your area is cleared."

Star of the Shift (SOTS)

Recognize the individual who showed the best performance during the session.

Dismissal Time

Once the Star of the Shift has been recognized, you must formally conclude the session. This is the final step where you thank the staff for their time and effort.

Objectives:

  • Formally end the shift session.
  • Encourage staff to attend future sessions.
  • Ensure all personnel are cleared from the shift room.

Final Dismissal Dialogue:

The Host must state the following to officially end the shift:

"You are officially dismissed. Thank you for your hard work and for attending this Greggs UK shift. We hope to see you at the next one!"
Note: After dismissal, ensure you log the shift in the appropriate channels if required by your department.
